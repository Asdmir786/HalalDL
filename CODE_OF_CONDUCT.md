# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

HalalDL is a small project, but the goal is still a respectful and useful community.

Examples of behavior that help:

- Being kind and constructive
- Giving specific feedback without personal attacks
- Assuming good intent while still being honest
- Making room for beginners and people asking basic questions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, discussions, or pull requests

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation that harms the community.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, email the maintainer listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) or open a private channel if one is available.

This document is intentionally lightweight and follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
